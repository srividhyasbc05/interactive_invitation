About Me

My name is Srividhya and I began learning bharatanatyam, at the age of 6, under the guidance of Smt. Janani Narayanan, Artistic Director of Nrityakalya Dance Company in the Bay Area, California. As a part of Nrityakalya Dance Company, I have been a part of their major dance productions, and participated in numerous dance competitions both with my group and solo. After completing my arangetram in 2019, I participated in numerous workshops under various gurus including Lakshman Sir. In 2023 I began learning under Acharya Choodamani Guru A. Lakshmanaswamy through online classes and under him, completed my second solo debut in Chennai "Nrityamalika". I am currently continuing classes under Lakshman Sir and is also spearheading the marketing for the dance team at NYU called NYU Laya, NYU's first Indian Classical dance group. I have been part of IndianRaga and the IDIA festival in the Bay Area and have won numerous competitions prior to college while completing my diploma in bharatnatyam through University of Silicon Andhra. I am currently a student at NYU Tandon School of Engineering studying Integrated Design and Media with minors in Business of Entertainment and Media Technologies and Interactive Media Arts.

About my Guru

Shri A. Lakshmanaswamy started his dancing career with initial training from Smt. Meera Venugopal of Bharata Kalanjali, Malaysia. At Chennai, he was guided by Smt. K.J. Sarasa, Prof. C. V. Chandrasekar and Smt. Kalanidhi Narayanan - all doyens in the art of Bharatanatyam. Guru Lakshmanaswamy has performed in all the prestigious Sabhas in Chennai and at numerous dance festivals in India and overseas. He has been acclaimed as the best male Bharatanatyam dancer by many Natyacharyas in the field and the media. Guru Lakshmanaswamy has received many prestigious awards like the Acharaya Chudamani from Shri Krishna Gana Sabha, Madura Kalamani from Karthik Fine Arts, Bharatha Kala Rathna, Yuva Kala Bharati, Natanamani, Swayam Prakasha Narthaka, Sathya Acharya Sundaram and Acharya Kala Vipanchi and many more. He was also bestowed the Narthaka award by Natyanjali trust and dance guru award at the 27th annual "spirit of youth festival" held at the Music Academy in August 2015. A performer par excellence, Guru Lakshmanaswamy's passion for the art form and his endeavour to teach has given his Sishyas a deep sense of respect, thus molding them into better dancers.
